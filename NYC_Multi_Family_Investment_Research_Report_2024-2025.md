# NYC Multi-Family Home Investment Research Report
## Neighborhoods Under $900K | 2024-2025 Analysis

**Report Date:** November 19, 2025
**Investment Criteria:** Multi-family homes (2-4 units) under $900,000
**Minimum Grade:** B- or better neighborhoods only
**Coverage:** All 5 NYC Boroughs

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

### Market Overview

The NYC multi-family market in 2024-2025 presents compelling opportunities for investors seeking properties under $900K, particularly in the outer boroughs. With vacancy rates at historic lows (2.8%), rental demand remaining strong, and average rents increasing 1.7% year-over-year, the fundamentals support long-term investment potential.

**Key Market Metrics:**
- **Total NYC Multi-Family Inventory:** 1,558 properties on StreetEasy, 1,882 on PropertyShark
- **Cap Rates:** 4.74% (A-class), 4.92% (B-class), 5.38% (C-class/Value-add)
- **Vacancy Rate:** 2.8% citywide (exceptionally low)
- **Rent Growth:** +1.7% YoY (2024)
- **5-Year Rent Forecast:** +3.1% annually (above pre-pandemic 2.7% average)

### Investment Landscape by Borough

**THE BRONX:** The clear leader for sub-$900K multi-family investments with median prices of $375K and 422 available properties. Offers the best value proposition with strong rental demand and improving infrastructure.

**QUEENS:** Mixed pricing with pockets of affordability, particularly in eastern Queens. Median prices range from $345K-$875K depending on neighborhood. Strong commuter access and diverse tenant base.

**BROOKLYN:** Limited options under $900K, with median multi-family prices at $1.55M. Only specific neighborhoods in southern and eastern Brooklyn offer opportunities in the target range.

**STATEN ISLAND:** Some opportunities exist, particularly on the North Shore, but inventory is limited. Properties under $900K available in select areas.

**MANHATTAN:** No viable options under $900K for multi-family properties.
